Cashew Sour Cream

Description:

This plant-based classic sour cream can be used as it is, as a great base for a plant-based mayo or to thicken dressings and sauces.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups raw cashews, soaked in water to soften
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • Water to blend
  1. In a high speed blender, blend all ingredients until thick and smooth. Drizzle a little bit of water at a time to just enable blending.
  2. Can be stored in the refrigerator for up to a week.
